WEB DEVLOPMENT

Web development is the process of building and maintaining websites and web applications for the internet or private intranets. It involves technical and creative tasks, including coding, design, and database management, using languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The field is divided into front-end (what the user sees), back-end (server-side logic), and full-stack (both) development.

APP DEVLOPMENT

App development is the process of designing, coding, testing, and deploying software applications for various platforms, most commonly mobile devices. It involves multiple phases, from initial planning and design to development, testing, and eventual release and maintenance. The process requires a diverse team of professionals, including programmers, designers, and project managers, to create apps that solve problems or provide value to users
